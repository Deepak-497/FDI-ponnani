THE DRIVE

In the recent floods that devastated Kerala's geography as well as economy, apart from the nearly 400 lives lost, Ponnani Legislative Assembly constituency in Malappuram district envisaged a special method to calculate the intensity of the calamity that the area faced. A Geographic Information System (GIS) Drone and Field Survey was carried out here from August 23 to 30 under the leadership of Kerala Legislative Assembly Speaker P Sreeramakrishnan, who is also the MLA of Ponnani.

KEY POINTS OF ANALYSIS

GIS DRONE SURVAILLANCE

The drone based aerial survey with an altitude of 100-300 metres could penetrate up to 4cm. These footages, captured from the flood-affected areas could be further rectified, with               an accuracy of 3 millimetres. This is done through 24 marked                   points in radius of 7km from base point by a measure of 5mm                       horizontal and 8mm vertical.

REAL-TIME FIELD ANALYSIS &
DATA VERIFICATION

Real-Time field survey was carried out by using mobile application. Telephonic conversation was made continuously for 10 hours a day over by Call Management System to verify                     the source of destruction (deconstruction/devastation).
